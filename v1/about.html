ARTIST STATEMENT // PURPOSE:

Liam is an artist who simply must create, he is also confused why he’s writing about himself in the third-person. Anyway... Every day, I wake up inspired by the colors of the sky, the sounds of the universe, and the infinite possibilities of my own ambition. My art is motivated by my soul, dreams, and caffeine. I love when people connect with my work, which can be described as a collaboration between my imagination and their metaphorical-metaphysical perception of reality. I wish to continue learning new skills that will broaden my potential of what I can create, and further my goals. My current goal is to learn how to properly use the ctrl+s shortcut before my programs crash. I would also like to learn how to cook yummy pizza.
